## 12. Service Worker Onboarding

### 12.1 Onboarding Process (Your Contacts)

**Step 1: Initial Contact** (Week 1)
- Explain platform to service workers you know
- Show them the commission (75%)
- Highlight benefits (steady work, payments on time, ratings)

**Step 2: Platform Setup** (Week 1-2)
- Simple registration on your app/website
- Basic profile creation
- Photo verification
- Bank details for payment

**Step 3: First Booking** (Week 2-3)
- Platform directs customer to them
- They execute service
- Customer rates and pays on platform
- Payment settled to their bank

**Step 4: Scaling** (Month 2+)
- Word-of-mouth among service providers
- They refer other service workers
- Your network grows organically

**Your Advantage:** You already have these contacts! No cold outreach needed.
