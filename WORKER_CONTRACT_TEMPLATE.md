### 4. Quality & conduct
- You are solely responsible for the quality and safety of the work you perform.
- You will behave courteously and lawfully, respect customers' homes and property, and
  carry valid ID.
- Customers rate each job. Repeated low ratings, complaints, or misconduct may lead to
  suspension or removal from the Platform.

### 5. Disputes
Customer complaints are handled by the Platform's dispute process (review of the booking
record, payment, and ratings). You agree to cooperate and, where a complaint is upheld,
to a re-service or an adjustment to your payout as reasonably determined by the Platform.

### 6. Verification
You confirm the ID and details you provided are true. You consent to the Platform
verifying your identity and contact details and storing them securely for payouts and
fraud prevention, in line with our Privacy Policy.

### 7. Independent liability
You are responsible for any loss, injury, or damage you cause while performing services.
The Platform acts only as an intermediary connecting you with customers and is not liable
for your acts or omissions.

### 8. Term & termination
Either party may end this Agreement at any time with notice. Pending completed bookings
will still be paid out. Sections 1, 3 (for completed work), 6, and 7 survive termination.

### 9. Governing law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of India and subject to the jurisdiction of the
courts at Chhindwara, Madhya Pradesh.
