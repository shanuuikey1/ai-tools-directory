## 6. Break-Even Analysis

### 6.1 Break-Even Calculation

**Break-Even Point (Monthly):**
- Monthly Operating Expenses: ₹11,09,000 (Phase 3 average)
- Commission Margin: 19% (average)
- Required Monthly Revenue to Break-Even:

```
Break-Even Revenue = Operating Expenses / Commission Margin
                   = ₹11,09,000 / 0.19
                   = ₹58,36,842 per month
```

**Required Monthly Services to Break-Even:**
```
Monthly Services = Break-Even Revenue / Avg Service Value
                 = ₹58,36,842 / ₹750
                 = 7,782 services per month
```
