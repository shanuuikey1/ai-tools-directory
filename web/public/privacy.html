Privacy Policy

Ghar Pahuch Seva — Chhindwara

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Ghar Pahuch Seva ("we", "us", "our") operates the Ghar Pahuch Seva mobile app and website (the "Service"), a marketplace that connects customers in Chhindwara with home-service professionals. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details: name, email address, phone number and password.
  • Booking details: service address, date/time, and the services you book.
  • Payment information: processed securely by our payment partner (Razorpay). We do not store your full card or bank details on our servers.
  • Service provider details: for workers — name, contact, service categories, and bank/UPI details used solely for payouts.
  • Usage data: basic technical data such as device type and app interactions, used to keep the Service working and secure.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account and bookings.
  • To connect you with service professionals and complete jobs.
  • To process payments and pay service providers.
  • To provide customer support and send service-related updates.
  • To improve the Service and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Sharing your information

We share only what is necessary to deliver the Service:

  • With the service professional assigned to your booking (your name, address and contact, so they can reach you and perform the job).
  • With our payment processor (Razorpay) to handle payments.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account at any time (see Contact).

5. Security

Passwords are stored hashed, and payment data is handled by a PCI-compliant processor. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

6. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information, and withdraw consent, by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable time.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
