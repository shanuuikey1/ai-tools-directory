## 1. The Math (Why Month 1 Profitability is Possible)

### 1.1 Break-Even Calculation

**Monthly Operating Costs:**
```
Internet & Phone:        ₹3,000
Hosting/Server:          ₹500
Payment Gateway Fees:    ₹2,000
SMS/Email Service:       ₹500
Software Tools:          ₹1,500
Insurance (prorated):    ₹800
Miscellaneous:           ₹500
─────────────────────────────
TOTAL MONTHLY:           ₹10,300
```

**Your Commission Per Service:**
- Average service value: ₹700
- Your commission (25%): **₹175 per service**

**Break-Even Services Needed:**
```
Break-Even = Monthly Costs / Commission per Service
           = ₹10,300 / ₹175
           = 59 services needed

With 100 services = ₹7,200 profit ✅
```

**That's it!** You just need **59-100 services in Month 1** to be profitable.
